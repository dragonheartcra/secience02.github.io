Innovation

Our innovation combines an AI-trained model with sensors embedded in a pillow to monitor elderly health data while they are lying down. Specifically, we focus on detecting potential heart problems, which are more likely to occur during the late night when elderly individuals are typically unsupervised. By using our product, we aim to provide early detection of these issues without disturbing the elder's sleep, offering peace of mind to both the elderly and their caregivers. Our innovation is particularly timely because it addresses the gap in care during the night, which is when elderly individuals are most vulnerable. We believe that not only families of elderly individuals will find our innovation valuable, but nursing homes will also embrace it as it helps reduce the immense pressure on caregivers. With its potential to address a pressing need, our innovation has great opportunities to spread.
